The Feedback Results (ئەنجامەکانى فیدباک) module allows lecturers to access and review student evaluations of their performance and the subjects they teach. This data is critical for academic quality assurance and personal professional development.

## 1. Accessing Feedback Results

To view your evaluations, click on **Feedback Results (ئەنجامەکانى فیدباک)** in the top navigation bar. The page consolidates data from different academic years and specific modules.

![Feedback Results Page]()

## 2. Yearly Performance Summary

At the top of the page, the system provides a high-level summary of your performance over time. This table, titled **"Student's Feedback Result for the Teacher by Year"**, tracks your growth:

- **Year (ساڵ):** Lists the academic years (e.g., 2023-2024, 2024-2025).
- **Total Average (کۆى گشتى):** Displays your overall score for that year (e.g., 3.9 or 4.2).

![Yearly Performance Summary Table]()

## 3. Detailed Subject Feedback

The second section, **"Student's Feedback Result for Subject and Teacher"**, breaks down evaluations by specific modules. This helps you identify which subjects were most effectively delivered.

### Table Columns

- **Subject (بابەت):** The name of the module (e.g., Computer Principles, Electronic Marketing).
- **Department (بەش):** The academic department where the module was taught.
- **College (کۆلێژ):** The associated college (e.g., Administrative Technical College).
- **Result (ئەنجام):** The specific score received for that module.
- **Year (ساڵ):** The academic cycle the feedback belongs to.

![Detailed Subject Feedback Table]()

## 4. Qualitative Student Comments

The final section of the page, **"Written Notes for Subject and Teacher"**, provides qualitative insights. These are direct comments submitted by students regarding your teaching style and the course structure.

- **Feedback Examples:** Comments range from simple praise like "Bash" (Good) and "Zor basha" (Very Good) to detailed descriptions such as "organized, explains with peace and smile, amazing teacher."
- **Usage:** These notes provide context to the numerical scores and help lecturers understand specific student needs or successes.

![Student Written Comments Section]()

## 5. Administrative Actions

- **Language Toggle:** Like the rest of the system, you can view the labels in Kurdish or English.
- **Printing:** Use the yellow **Print** button at the top right to generate a physical or PDF report of your feedback results for your academic portfolio.
